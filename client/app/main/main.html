Belief in Motion

Is an organization that is trying to change the lives of Roma Gypsies through empowerment and education.

We are building schools for education. We are providing transportation for those who don't have access to high school.

We are empowering women in the community by giving them jobs and skills that keep them from resorting to prostitution.
